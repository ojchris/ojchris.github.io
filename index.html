Oconn Systems solutions has served well over 2,000,000 people (client board/management, employees, vendors/applicants as well as government and citizens), in the next decade (from 2019) I hope to be part of or to create ambitious consumer-focused projects that will help to serve the needs of many: possibly a billion people. To achieve this I'm required to learn new emerging fields such as Big Data and AI while mastering so-called difficult options in Project, People and Enterprise Architecture and Management, Drupal, SharePoint, etc.
